Date Sweetened Chocolate Truffles

When you need a quick pick-me-up

Sometimes you need a little something sweet after a meal but don't want a "full-sized" dessert. These cute little truffles are a great healthier dessert option that will satisfy your craving.

Ingredients:

  • 10 medjool dates, pitted (180 grams)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened caco powder (plus more for rolling)
  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons cashew butter (or other neutral nut butter)
  • 1 healthy pinch sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 - 3/4 cup almond flour

Steps:

  1. Line a dish with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Add pitted dates, oil, and water to a food processor. Mix until a paste forms.
  3. Add cacao powder, vanilla, cashew butter, and a healthy pinch of salt and blend again to combine.
  4. Then add the almond flour in 28 g measurements until the mixture forms a ball in the food processor.
  5. Scoop out 1-Tbsp amounts of the mixture and roll into balls.
  6. Set each ball on the parchment-lined dish.
  7. Optional: Add some cacao powder onto a plate and roll the truffles around to coat.
  8. You can enjoy them now or put them in the freezer for 30 minutes to firm them up a bit.
